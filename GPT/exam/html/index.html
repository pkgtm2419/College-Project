    • Our Vision

    Aryahans Educational Institute Pvt. Ltd. was established in the year 1995 to prepare the students for IIT(JEE) and other Engineering Entrance Examinations. Later Medical Entrance, NTSE, Olympiads and NDA, CDS were also added.

    Since its establishment the institute has produced wonderful results and has made its presence felt not only in Uttar Pradesh but in the entire country. This all could be possible only due to proper implementation of the planning, taking timely feedback from the students regarding their level of satisfaction at the institute and follow up action to plug the shortcomings from time to time. Within the period of its existence many students have been selected in IITs and various engineering colleges of repute through IIT(JEE) and other engineering entrance exams, have cleared NTSE, science and maths Olympiads and NDA. Engineering is a career option to build a nation in the literal sense of the term. The proposed project aims at imparting coaching training to students on the syllabus of the entrance examinations IIT-JEE (Indian Institutes of Technology, Joint Entrance Examination), AIEEE (All-India Engineering Entrance Examination) and UPSEAT (Lucknow). The students would get about an eleven month coaching and would also appear for the mock tests before the competitive examination begins.

    I believe that everyone has the power to transform his future. Besides the inherent intelligence, a student needs care, attention, guidance and quality coaching to perform in the competitive era. If you treat the students the way they are, you make them worse. However, if you treat the students the way they could be, you make them better. It has been my observation that most people get ahead during the time others waste. Actually, success consists of a series of little daily efforts. If you want to be successful in life then you must believe in action. The focus of National level competitions such as IIT- JEE has shifted towards applied aspects, so for success clear concepts and rigorous practice on relevant modules is required from the very beginning to take an unbeatable lead. I’ve always felt that in doing great things, self-confidence is the foremost requirement. Whatever you do, do it with full confidence. It is the key to success.

    Er. Anil Singh

    B.Tech(Elec)
